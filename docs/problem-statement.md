# Problem Statement

**Theme:** Information Integrity

Kenyan voters — particularly Gen Z and rural populations — are exposed to AI-manipulated political content on WhatsApp and TikTok with no accessible way to verify it at the point of contact. Existing fact-checking tools are desktop-based, English-heavy, and unreachable by the communities most vulnerable to manipulation.

Hakiki solves this by providing claim verification and AI-media detection directly inside WhatsApp, SMS, and USSD — in Swahili, English, or Sheng — with zero registration and zero personal data stored.
